URGENT: Add Email to Render Dashboard
=====================================

Your .env file is LOCAL ONLY - it's NOT on Render!

You MUST add these to Render Dashboard:

1. Go to: https://dashboard.render.com/
2. Click: bottleji-api service
3. Click: "Environment" tab
4. Add:
   EMAIL_USER = your-email@gmail.com
   EMAIL_PASS = ojwi qbpy kbcs zplx
5. Save - Render will auto-redeploy
