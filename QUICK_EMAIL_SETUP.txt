QUICK SETUP: Enable Email Service
==================================

1. GET GMAIL APP PASSWORD:
   - Go to: https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
   - Generate app password for "Mail" → "Other (Custom name)" → "Bottleji Backend"
   - Copy the 16-character password

2. ADD TO RENDER:
   - Go to: https://dashboard.render.com/
   - Select your backend service
   - Go to "Environment" tab
   - Add:
     EMAIL_USER=your-email@gmail.com
     EMAIL_PASS=your-16-char-app-password
   - Save (auto-redeploys)

3. VERIFY:
   - Check Render logs for: "✅ Email service initialized successfully"
   - Test by signing up a new user

That's it! Email service will be functional.
