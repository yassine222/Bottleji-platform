# SendGrid Single Sender Form - How to Fill

## Form Fields Guide

Here's what to put in each field:

---

## Required Fields

### **From Name**
- **What to put**: `Bottleji` or `Bottleji App`
- **Example**: `Bottleji`
- **Purpose**: This is what recipients see as the sender name

### **From Email Address**
- **What to put**: Your Gmail address (or any email you own)
- **Example**: `bottleji.app@gmail.com` or `your-email@gmail.com`
- **Purpose**: This is the email address that will send emails
- **Important**: You must be able to access this email to verify it

### **Reply To**
- **What to put**: Same as From Email Address (or leave blank)
- **Example**: `bottleji.app@gmail.com` (same as From Email)
- **Purpose**: Where replies will go
- **Tip**: Usually same as From Email

---

## Address Fields

### **Company Address (Line 1)**
- **What to put**: Your address (can be personal or business)
- **Example**: `123 Main Street` or `Your Home Address`
- **Purpose**: Required by SendGrid for compliance
- **Note**: Can be your personal address if you don't have a business

### **Company Address (Line 2)**
- **What to put**: Optional - leave blank or add apartment/unit number
- **Example**: `Apt 4B` or leave blank
- **Purpose**: Additional address info (optional)

### **City**
- **What to put**: Your city
- **Example**: `Tunis` or `New York`
- **Purpose**: Required address field

### **State**
- **What to put**: Your state/province
- **Example**: `Tunis` or `New York` or `California`
- **Purpose**: Required address field
- **Note**: If you don't have states in your country, use your region

### **Zip Code**
- **What to put**: Your postal/zip code
- **Example**: `1000` or `10001`
- **Purpose**: Required address field

### **Country**
- **What to put**: Your country
- **Example**: `Tunisia` or `United States`
- **Purpose**: Required address field

---

## Optional Fields

### **Nickname**
- **What to put**: Optional - a name to identify this sender
- **Example**: `Bottleji Production` or `Main Sender`
- **Purpose**: Just for your reference in SendGrid dashboard
- **Note**: You can leave this blank

---

## Complete Example

Here's a complete example form:

```
From Name: Bottleji
From Email Address: bottleji.app@gmail.com
Reply To: bottleji.app@gmail.com
Company Address: 123 Main Street
Company Address Line 2: (leave blank)
City: Tunis
State: Tunis
Zip Code: 1000
Country: Tunisia
Nickname: Bottleji Production (optional)
```

---

## Quick Tips

1. **From Email**: Use an email you can access (you'll need to verify it)
2. **From Name**: Keep it simple and professional
3. **Address**: Can be your personal address - it's just for compliance
4. **Reply To**: Usually same as From Email
5. **Nickname**: Optional - just for your reference

---

## After Filling the Form

1. Click **"Create"** or **"Next"**
2. Check your email inbox (the From Email Address you entered)
3. Click the verification link in the email
4. ✅ **Done!** Your sender is verified

---

## Common Questions

### "Do I need a real business address?"
- No, you can use your personal address
- SendGrid just needs it for compliance/legal reasons
- It won't be shown to email recipients

### "What if I don't have a company?"
- That's fine! Use your personal address
- "Company Address" can be your home address

### "Can I use a Gmail address?"
- Yes! Gmail works perfectly
- Just make sure you can access it to verify

### "What if I make a mistake?"
- You can edit or delete the sender later
- Or create a new one with correct info

---

## That's It!

Fill in the form with your information, verify your email, and you're ready to send emails! 🎉
